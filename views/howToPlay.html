Trivia Hub is a collection of Trivia questions to answer for FUN!

You can also Create your own CUSTOM QUESTIONS with the CREATE QUIZ option!

    How to play...
  1. Click on Trivia Hub at the top of the page
  2. Select your trivia category
  3. Click Start Game
  4. The GOAL is to SCORE as many points as possible before you run out of LIVES
  5. You will only have 3 LIVES! Everytime you answer incorrectly you lose a life
  6. After each game you can POST your SCORE to see where you rank with others
  7. Have FUN and GOOD LUCK!
